> Official Vatly packages for your language or framework.

Vatly provides official packages to integrate billing, subscriptions, and tax compliance into your application.

## Available packages

| Language / Framework | Package | Install |
| --- | --- | --- |
| PHP 8.0+ | [vatly-api-php](/packages/php/readme) | `composer require vatly/vatly-api-php` |
| Laravel 11+ | [vatly-laravel](/packages/laravel/readme) | `composer require vatly/vatly-laravel` |
